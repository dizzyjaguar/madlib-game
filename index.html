Concerning Pipe-weed There is another astonishing thing about Hobbits of old that must be mentioned, a/an astonishing habit: they imbibed or inhaled, through pipes of clay or wood , the smoke of the burning leaves of a herb, which they called pipe-weed or leaf , a variety probably of Nicotiana or Marijuana. A great deal of mystery surrounds the origin of this peculiar custom, or 'art' as the Hobbits preferred to call it. All that could be discovered about it in antiquity was put together by Brandybuck (later Master of Buckland ), and since he and the tobacco of the Southfarthing play a part in the history that follows, his remarks in the introduction to his Herblore of the Shire may be quoted.
